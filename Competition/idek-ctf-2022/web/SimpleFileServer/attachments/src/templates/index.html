SimpleFileServer

A very simple website to host your very simple files on the cloud, for free.

Features:

  • No file inspecting: we don't care what you upload, unless it's actually illegal
  • Permalinks: unlike some other sites, your files won't get deleted due to inactivity
  • Totally anonymous: people going to your files won't know who the uploader is
At this point in time, we only support 2MB zipped uploads, but we're hoping to increase this limit in the future when bandwidth isn't as expensive anymore.
